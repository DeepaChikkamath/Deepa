Deepa Chikkamath

I am a fifth year BS-MS student at IISER Mohali majoring in Physics. I started with interest in astrophysics, but wanted to explore other fields. So I worked in condensed matter physics, experimental quantum optics, fluid dynamics, classical and quantum turbulence. I have pursued research internships in these fields at IISER Mohali, IIT Dharwad, Côte d'Azur Observatory - Nice.

In July 2025, I began my master’s thesis at Laboratoire PMMH, ESPCI Paris under the guidance of Prof. Stéphane Perrard. This work is funded by CNRS and the Indo-Thai Fellowship organised by the Fonds ESPCI in collaboration with Michelin Corporate Foundation.

These amazing experiences have led me to develop keen interest in superconductivity, quantum fluids.

Outside of physics, I enjoy travelling, taking long walks, hiking, playing chess and I love cooking. With deep appreciation for art, Paris has fed my hunger with its museums and art galleries.
